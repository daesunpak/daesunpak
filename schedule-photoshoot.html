HOW TO SCHEDULE YOUR LISTING'S PHOTO SHOOT

Snap2Close uses an easy and convenient process to schedule a photo shoot for your listing. The Client Center has three basic components.

The Account Creator section requires first time users to create an account. This section is where you will be able to upload your headshot photo and business logo, enter your address, phone number, website information, broker’s name, and add an assistant’s name and email address if you use one. Once you have created an account you will be able to login at anytime without the need to enter this information again to order a new photoshoot. Once you click on the "Click here to start" button a the bottom of this screen you will then be taken to our Client Center which exists on a separate server. To start there you will need to scroll down to the bottom of it's screen and click the large white bar with "Sign Up & Start a Project Today"

The Create a New Project section is where you will order a new photo shoot, select which real estate photography package you want to order and add any extras such as a twilight shoot or aerial (drone) photos. You will also be able to pick the time and date your want from our real-time schedular which shows you available time slots based upon your location, the home's square footage and the package and options you wish to purchase.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Photographing the best features of a home will be impacted by the direction the home faces and the position of the sun. If you house faces due West then you would probably want to schedule your photo shoot for the afternoon so that the sun will fully illuminate the front of the house. However, if listing's best interior rooms have large windows facing West then you might want to consider something more along mid-day when the sun is at it's peak.

Your listing’s Project/Dashboard section is where you will download your new photos, edit and create your branded and non-branded MLS compliant slideshow/tours and your property’s new webpage. To schedule your first project then click the NEW PROJECT text on the banner at the top right hand side of the screen.
